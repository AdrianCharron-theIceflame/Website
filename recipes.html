Recipes:

Our free to use recipes!

Seas Salt Ice Cream - Kingdom Hearts
This is a fan-favourite of the Kingdom Hearts series. Ever since its first appearance in Kingdom Hearts 2, this has been a sought-after recipe. Its “Salty, no… Sweat!” flavour, as Donald would say, is iconic and if you want a different you want a different take on the recipe consider giving our friends at the Babish Culinary Universe a shot!
Ultralisk Filet
Starcraft is an iconic game franchise known the world over. This alien race in particular is very fleshy: The Zerg. If anyone can manage to kill one of these behemoth-sized Zerg, also called an Ultralisk, has amazing cuts of meat associated with it. Well worth the risk, the filet is amazing!

It must be said that Gamer Foodz does not support the workplace culture that Activision Blizzard King has cultivated throughout the years.
Scrin Rings
These delectable little rings of golden batter and alien calamari are amazing! If you're going out hunting for a Scrin Assimilator though, bring a de-cloaking device and watch out for ambushes since they can't defend themselves. Sure, they may be Tiberium-based light forms, but for good food? Who wouldn't want to risk death by Tiberium exposure?
Spartan Salad
Do you think a Spartan God has gotten this big by eating only meat? Absolutely not! A Spartan God of War knows that a balanced meal must include even salads! Gluten-free, lactose-free and vegan-friendly as all salads in the Nordic and Greek countries should be!
Aunt May's Pancakes
Aunt May's Pancakes took the internet by storm when Spider-Man 2018 came out and for good reason. These are a healthy tasty version of your favourite household pancake recipe with a bonus blueberry coulis. Aunt May would be proud!
Mushroom Stew
Simple and flavourful! Multiple types of mushrooms and a full day of simmering in your oven will yield an absolutely scrumptious and delightfully meat-free stew.
Cinnamon Butterscotch Pie
Ah, Toriel who wouldn't want to enjoy a slice of your cinnamon butterscotch pie? Enjoy the smooth richness of the butterscotch mixed with the warming heat of the cinnamon. One can always tell when this pie is baked with motherly love!
Cocktail of Soul
If you're the Ghost of Hallownest, you'll need to stay as healthy as you can at all times to keep your Geo on you! Make a few of these Cocktails of Soul to share with you and your friends during a night of partying. For those who don't want any Radiance in your cocktails, we have an alcohol-free recipe too!
Birthday Headshot Doughnuts
Created with special guests Nic and Felix from FAN Industries, each doughnut is shot through with a precision rifle which causes an explosion of fine edible glitter in the studio to decorate our lovely treats. Enjoy the feeling of being as precise as some of the best soldiers in the year 2552!
