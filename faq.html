FAQ

I have [insert food restriction here]. Do you have any alternatives?

We at Gamer Foodz understand the diversity of our fan base as well as the diversity of any game's fan base. To be as inclusive as possible, we've chosen our recipes and ingredients to contain the least amount of allergens as possible. We also include local alternatives or alternative recipes where we can. If you cannot use a recipe at all, we also include suggestions for other recipes based on the game franchise and food style.

When are recipes added?

Recipes are added over time as we partner up with more and more games. As we get more visitors to our website and more purchases of books and merch we increase our reputation in the eyes of major video-game publishers and minor indie developer studios. Then, our Head Chef and Studio Relations work tirelessly with those companies until a foolproof recipe is devised!

How can I provide feedback on recipes?

On our Contac Us page, you can find a form to submit any kind of question, comment, review, or concern to us. Let us know if you found a simpler way to make the same recipe! Tell us about your favourite dish! Ask us a question so that we might add it here! Tell us what you think of Gamer Foodz!

How can I get my game's food on your website?

If you're a game dev, submit your company's contact information on our Contact Us form so we can get back to you. You'll be contacted by our Studio Relations member Kidane. If you're just interested in seeing your favourite game on our website, you can also submit a suggestion through the same form. We're always looking for new franchises to join our family!
