Meet The Team!

Creator: Adrian Charron

Adrian Created this website for his love of gaming and food. His father didn't start off as the best chef in the world, for a young Adrian, but improved to the point where his son would consider him a great home chef.
His father is also the one to have introduced Adrian to gaming. From a young age, Adrian Charron would play educational games like “Plumo at the Zoo”. He was then shown how to play games like “Age of Empires”, “Starcraft”, and “Command & Conquer: Tiberian Sun”.
These two loves blossomed into this website.

Studio Relations: Kidane

Kidane is in charge of contacting and keeping in contact with different game studios and companies that we would like to partner up with, or have partnered up with in the past.

Head Chef: Reggie

As the Head Chef, Reggie's knowledge of the games, and knowledge of cooking, is crucial to our company. His understanding of flavours is what makes our recipes one of a kind.

Head of Customer Support: Oscar

Oscar is the head of our Customer support department. That section of our team may be small, but to get you all the service you need, they will move mountains!

Design Team Lead Miguel

Miguel's skills with design are an inspiration to all of us on the team. Whether it's the design for our website, or our cookbook pictures and design elements, Miguel has had a hand in everything.
